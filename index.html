This site exists to provide you information on your MP's to inform your voting and provide any references to their stances on trans rights.

The site is not affiliated with any political party or organisation and I am not endorsing any candidate or party on the site. As I know someone will question it, this site is run independently by a trans person in their own time. I aim to keep this site poltically neutral and based on facts from offical sources but of course I might make mistakes so please do reach out if you see any.
We also utilise several 3rd party services to provide the map and data on this site, of which you can find more information on the specifics here: github.com/Atmois/transmps#3rd-party-licenses.

If you have any corrections, additions or improvements you can submit them on the GitHub repo or you can email me at zoe@transmps.uk.
Also if you're going to send me hate speech, don't even bother, you will be unconditionally blocked and sent this wonderful song.

Right now the site is focused on tracking EDM240, but I am working on making a tracking system for MP stances and statements on trans rights, in the meantime you are able to submit any references to MP stances and statements on the GitHub repo linked above in preperation for addition once it is implemented. I'm also looking into if I can make a live feed of MP updates on Bluesky so if you are able to help me figure that out, please reach out!

To provide extra data or sources and information on MP stances, please ensure you can back it up with a citation, ideally a Hansard Transcript or evidence from a reputable outlet. This is to ensure this site can remain as factual as possible and be used to the public on their MP's stances and provide as a reputable reference source. If you know of a statment but cannot find a source, reach out and I can help you find it or provide a source for it, but I will not add any references or information without a reputable source to prevent the spread of misinformation.

Now a little bit about the site, I saw EDM240 and thought "wouldn't it be nice to visualise this and have some party stats" so I bought the domain transmps.uk and made this site! I do not make any money off the site, nor do I ever plan to, but if you want to support the site, you can do so by sharing the site to educate others about our representives stances on trans rights and spread awareness of the challanges we face in our daily lives.
